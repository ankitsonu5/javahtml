What we do

Rescue

We rescue the sick, abandoned and homeless from the streets of Bangalore. A 24/7-helpline number +91 97395 44444, helps us identify patients/individuals who are in dire need of medical care and emotional support. Our ambulances along with members of our service team pick up such individuals from reported locations. Some of our residents are also sent from other NGO homes, shelters, or even brought to the home by their families who are unable to care for them.

Treatment

All those who are rescued, referred to us or brought to us are admitted at the A.i.R. Charitable Home. In case urgent medical treatment is required, we admit them at hospitals in the city. Post treatment, they are brought back to the home and looked after. We leave no stone unturned to give every patient a second chance at life and happiness.


Transformation

We ensure that our residents receive the requisite medical treatment through our partnering hospitals and provide them with counselling, personal attention and emotional support that paves the way for a gradual but discernible and visible transformation in them. Others are thankful for the free medical treatment and care that they receive, which helps them regain their lost health, strength and hope, thereby giving them an opportunity to live a normal life again.

Rehabilitation

Once our patients regain good health, and are deemed physically and mentally fit, they are shifted to other NGO homes, whether it is a shelter for women, the elderly or for homeless children. Some are sent back to the NGO homes that they were sent from; others are sent back to their villages or homes to be reunited their families. A few continue to be with us – happy to be a part of the A.i.R. Humanitarian Homes family. Some actively seek employment within our organization.
