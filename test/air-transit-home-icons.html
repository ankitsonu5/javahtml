Our Residents – Special Cases

While some residents stay at the home for a brief period of time till they recover or are rehabilitated, there are a few who will continue to live at the home as long as they live. These residents have been abandoned by their biological families – they have no roof over their heads and no one to care for them. They have been adopted by the A.i.R. Transit Home and are considered as a part of the A.i.R. Humanitarian Homes extended family. For these residents, the home is truly a home.
